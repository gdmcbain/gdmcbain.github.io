Spherical enclosures

The two mechanisms for producing spanwise flow proposed by Mallinson and de Vahl Davis (1973, 1977) are illuminated here by considering the flow in a spherical enclosure subject to horizontal vapour mass fraction and temperature gradients. Apart from the importance of spherical enclosures as part of closed porous materials, the results fully support the contentions of the previous section.
