Conclusions on the use of Fastflo

The principal advantages of Fastflo for this problem are:

  • It handles the unusual terms arising from the mass transfer effects.
  • It allows the use of alternative solution algorithms.
  • The simplicity and generality of the postprocessing procedures allows easy investigation and presentation of the results, including unusual transformations of the results, such as the `heat-function'.


Geordie McBain 2001-01-27
